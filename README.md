# ☕ Cafe Business Intelligence & Growth Strategy
An analysis of 17,000+ transactions to identify market trends and design a high-impact revenue campaign.

## 📌 Project Overview
The goal of this project was to move beyond raw data to provide actionable business recommendations for a cafe chain. I utilized BigQuery SQL to analyze customer loyalty, regional performance, and product pairings.

## 🚀 Key Business Insights
* **The "Single-Item Trap":** Data revealed that **95.7%** of customers purchase only one item.
* **Market Disparity:** **Pune** leads in volume (2,576 orders), but **Bangalore** is the premium market with a **$380 Average Order Value (AOV)**.
* **Loyalty Health:** Program penetration is sustainable at **8.14%**, showing a strong repeat customer base.

## 💡 Strategic Recommendation: "The Perfect Pair"
Based on the high volume of independent coffee and food sales, I designed a **"Second Item 50% Off"** campaign.

### Projected Financial Impact:
* **Coffee to Food Upsell:** +125,417 monthly revenue.
* **Food to Coffee Cross-sell:** +66,619 monthly revenue.
* **Total Estimated Lift:** **+192,036 per month.**

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
* **SQL:** BigQuery (Window functions, String manipulation, CTEs, Case Statements).
* **Data Visualization:** Excel (for trend analysis).
